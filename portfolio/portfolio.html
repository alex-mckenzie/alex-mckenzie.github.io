gentle stranger

about

Gentle Stranger is a collaborative project currently composed of members Alex McKenzie, Joshua Barfoot, and Tom Hardwick-Allan. Primarily operating through live performance and recorded music, Gentle Stranger released their debut album - which was recorded over a week in 2017 and featured Evangaline Ling, now of the band Audiobooks - on PRAH Recordings this year. Performances have taken place in a variety of venues such as Tate Modern, Southwark Park Gallery, Edel Assanti, Brixton Windmill, The Lexington and festivals including Supernormal, Green Man and Art Licks. Gentle Stranger have been featured in The Wire and The Quietus, the former of which they also created a mixtape for. They have collaborated with the artist Adam Christensen in creating music for his 2019 exhibition 'You're In My Veins' at Kunsthal Aarhus, and are currently working on a collaborative project with classical singer Siobhan Mooney.
