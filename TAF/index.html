~ The Amateur Falconer ~

- The Amateur Falconer has been trusted to trial the new prototype of the Raptorcam™ device

- Unfortunately, on the first test flight the falcon flew away and is showing no signs of returning

- Some information is being sent back from the Raptorcam™, which is presumably still attached to the falcon

- The rudimentary software is receiving transmissions containing GPS coordinates and some fragmented audio, which is being uploaded here

- Unfortunately the video doesn’t seem to be working, it’s not clear if the problem is with the Raptorcam™ (weather damage?) or with the software receiving the transmissions. Mother Dirt is looking into this
